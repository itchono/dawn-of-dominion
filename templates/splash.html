How to play

Dawn of Dominion is similar in design to Battleship. Two players place units down on their side of the game board at the beginning of the game. The objective of the game is to destroy all of the other player's units.

Key Mechanics

  1. Units have their own health bars and ATK/DEF stats
  2. Attacks made onto units will lower their health points until they reach zero, at which point they are destoyed.
  3. Enemy units are hidden until you land your first attack onto them, after which they are exposed
